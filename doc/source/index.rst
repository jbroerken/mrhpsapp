mrhpsapp
========
.. image:: banner.png
   :align: center
   
The mrhpsapp platform service handles events of the application category. 
The service reacts to launch requests (both timed and instant) and clearing 
launch requests.

Timed launches are stored by the service. This service will check and inform 
the current application about a timed launch and also send said timed launch.
